About Me

Hello! My name is Elizabeth A. Testerman. I am a Christian writer, teacher, and ministry leader passionate about helping believers engage lovingly and biblically with Jehovah’s Witnesses and individuals involved in other high-control religious groups.

My background includes theological training, apologetics, and years of experience teaching and mentoring Christians who want to grow in discernment and compassion. My goal is to equip the church to share the gospel clearly, confidently, and graciously.
